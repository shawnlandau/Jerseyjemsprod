Tournament Family Questions

Answers to the most common questions from visiting baseball families

Can I order before I arrive in Myrtle Beach?

Yes — and we strongly recommend it! Call or email us 2–7 days before your arrival. We'll confirm your order and have everything ready for pickup or delivery when you get here.

Do you serve team orders (10–20+ people)?

Absolutely. Team dinners, carb-load nights, and large breakfast packs are all available. Call us ahead of time and we'll quote you a custom team price and make sure everything is stocked and ready.

Do you deliver to condos and resorts?

Yes. Local delivery is available throughout Horry County, which covers most of the Myrtle Beach resort and condo areas. Call us to confirm your location and delivery fee.

How long does it actually take to cook?

Most meals are on the table in 10–15 minutes. Fresh pasta cooks in 3–5 minutes. Sauces heat in minutes. Sausage is quick in a skillet. We include cooking instructions with every order.

What if my condo doesn't have a big kitchen?

Most Myrtle Beach condos and villas have at least a two-burner stove. All you need is a pot for pasta and a pan for sausage. Our meals are designed to work with minimal kitchen space.

How long do the products last in the freezer?

All items are flash-frozen and will stay fresh in your condo freezer for 3–6 months. Perfect for stocking up on arrival and pulling out what you need each night. No rush to use everything at once.

How much does a typical family order cost?

Bundles start from around $28–$34 for a family dinner pack. A full week's worth of meals for a family of 4–5 typically runs $80–$150 depending on what you choose. Always cheaper than restaurants for the whole week.

How do I pay?

We accept cash, Venmo, Zelle, and all major credit cards at pickup. For delivery orders, payment can be arranged in advance. Team orders: we'll work out the best payment method for the group.
