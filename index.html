Frequently Asked Questions

Everything tournament families need to know about ordering

Can I order before I arrive in Myrtle Beach?

Absolutely — and we recommend it! Call or email ahead with your arrival date and what meals you need. We'll confirm your order and have everything ready when you get here. Just call 843-548-7730 or email hello@jerseyjemsmb.com.

Do you handle large team orders?

Yes! Team dinners, carb-load nights, and bulk pasta packs are all available. Just call us ahead of time so we can make sure we have everything in stock for your group size. We love feeding teams.

Where do I pick up my order?

We'll provide the exact pickup location when you place your order. It's in the Myrtle Beach area, easy to access. We also have a booth at Hudson's Market every Thursday at 7000 N. Kings Highway.

Can you deliver to my condo or resort?

Yes! We offer local delivery in Horry County. Call us to discuss delivery options and any fee that may apply based on your location. Great for families who want to stock up on arrival without making a detour.

How far in advance do I need to order?

Most items are available with 2–3 days notice. For larger team orders or special requests, calling at least a week ahead is ideal. For tournament families, we recommend ordering before you leave home.

How long does it take to cook?

Most meals take 10–15 minutes to prepare. Fresh pasta cooks in 3–5 minutes, sauces heat in minutes, and sausage is quick in a skillet. We'll include prep notes with every order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, Venmo, Zelle, and all major credit cards at pickup. For team orders, we're happy to work out the payment method that's easiest for the group.

How long do products stay frozen?

All items are flash-frozen and will stay fresh in your freezer for 3–6 months. Perfect for stocking a condo freezer on arrival and eating well all week. We'll include storage instructions with your order.

Still have questions? We'd love to help plan your tournament week meals.
