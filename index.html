Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering

Where do I pick up my order?

We'll provide the exact pickup location when you place your order. It's in the Myrtle Beach area, easy to access from Highway 17.

How far in advance do I need to order?

Most items are available with 2-3 days notice. For larger orders or special items, we recommend calling at least a week ahead.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, Venmo, Zelle, and all major credit cards at pickup.

Is there a minimum order?

No minimum! Whether you want one package of ravioli or stocking up for a family gathering, we're happy to help.

How long do products stay frozen?

All items are flash-frozen and will stay fresh in your freezer for 3-6 months. We'll provide specific storage instructions at pickup.

Do you deliver?

Currently we offer pickup only, but we're working on delivery options for the future. Call us to discuss special arrangements for large orders.

Still have questions?
