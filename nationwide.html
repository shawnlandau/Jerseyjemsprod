Nationwide Shipping FAQ

Everything you need to know about ordering online

Where do you ship?

We ship to all 50 states! Whether you're in California, Texas, Florida, or Alaska, we'll get authentic Italian specialties to your door frozen solid.

How long does shipping take?

We use 2-day express shipping (FedEx or UPS) to ensure everything arrives frozen. Most orders ship within 1-2 business days of being placed.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs vary by location and order size, typically $25-45. Exact cost is calculated at checkout. Bundle orders to maximize value!

Will my food arrive frozen?

Yes! We use professional insulated coolers and plenty of dry ice. Items arrive frozen solid. If anything arrives thawed, contact us immediately for a replacement.

What's the minimum order?

No minimum! However, we recommend ordering multiple items to make the most of your shipping cost. Bundles offer the best value.

What if I'm not home for delivery?

The packaging keeps items frozen for 24-48 hours. We recommend bringing packages inside as soon as possible. You can also provide delivery instructions at checkout.

What's your return policy?

If anything arrives damaged or thawed, contact us within 24 hours with photos. We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund immediately.

Can I schedule a specific delivery date?

We ship orders 1-2 days after placement. You can add delivery notes at checkout, but exact delivery dates depend on the carrier's schedule.
